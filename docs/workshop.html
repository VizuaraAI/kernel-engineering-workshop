Vizuara's Kernel Engineering Workshop

Eight live foundational lectures — each paired with a hands-on coding lab — six deep-dive workshops on modern topics from the three performance regimes to DeepSeek's DSpark and AI-generated kernels, and three CPU-parallelism sessions to close the loop back to the silicon. Enrolled students get the complete 72-chapter book, the GPU-Puzzles track and quizzes, the guided projects, worklog assignments, and the "You vs the machine" capstone.
